Pizza

Description

This pizza is a great idea for a cheap meal that still is high quality!

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Make the dough into a circle
  2. Put marinara sauce on the dough
  3. Put cheese on the pizza
  4. Put he pizza in the oven for 15 minutes
  5. Eat the pizza!
