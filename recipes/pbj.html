Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

Peanut Butter and Jelly is a great idea for a cheap meal that still is high quality!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Put peanut butte one one piece of bread
  2. Put jelly on another piece of bread
  3. Stack them
  4. Eat the peanut butter and jelly sandwich!
